# Google Business Profile content plan for Nautica

This is the practical growth layer that sits on top of the on-site SEO work.

## Non-negotiable profile consistency
Use the exact same business identity everywhere:
- Business name: Nautica
- Legal footer data stays on the website only where appropriate
- Address: DONJA RIVA 55, 22212 Tribunj, Croatia
- Main category: Cocktail bar
- Secondary category only if accurate: Cafe / Coffee shop / Bar
- Website: production website root URL
- Reservation URL: `/reservation`
- Phone: the same phone shown on the site and in footer

## Weekly execution rhythm

### Monday
- Upload 3 to 5 fresh photos:
  - terrace in daylight
  - coffee service
  - cocktails
  - sunset frame
  - evening atmosphere

### Wednesday
- Publish one GBP Update post:
  - sunset invitation
  - menu highlight
  - event reminder
  - terrace atmosphere

### Friday
- Publish one Event or Offer post if relevant:
  - DJ evening
  - sunset session
  - special weekend reservation push

### Sunday
- Review replies:
  - reply to every fresh review
  - mention context naturally: sunset, terrace, cocktails, coffee, service, Tribunj
  - do not spam keywords mechanically

## Review capture flow
- Train staff to ask only after a clearly positive moment.
- Best moments:
  - after sunset round
  - after event night
  - after group celebration
  - after visibly satisfied coffee/cocktail experience
- Keep the ask simple:
  - HR: "Ako vam je bilo lijepo, puno bi nam značila kratka Google recenzija."
  - EN: "If you enjoyed the experience, a short Google review would help us a lot."
- Use a QR code that opens the Google Maps / profile destination.

## Photo shot list
Capture and rotate:
- exterior / arrival / signage
- terrace wide shots
- cocktail closeups
- coffee closeups
- staff in service
- sunset atmosphere
- evening crowd without chaos
- event moments
- details that feel premium: glassware, light, sea reflections

## Review reply framework
- thank the guest
- mention one real detail from the review
- reinforce place context naturally
- invite them back

Example:
"Hvala vam na dolasku i na lijepim riječima. Drago nam je da su vam se svidjeli kokteli i atmosfera uz zalazak na rivi u Tribunju. Veselimo se novom dolasku."

## Questions & answers to keep ready
Add or prepare answers for common GBP questions:
- Do you take reservations?
- Is Nautica good for sunset drinks?
- Can we come just for coffee?
- Is Nautica a good stop if we stay in Vodice or Srima?
- Do you host events?
- Is there terrace seating?

## What not to do
- do not stuff the business name with keywords
- do not buy reviews
- do not copy/paste identical review replies
- do not post low-quality or outdated photos for months
- do not let hours drift away from real opening times
